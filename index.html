How to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps to delete your account from ClearMind:

📎

Step 1: Open the App

Launch the ClearMind application on your device.

📅

Step 2: Go to Moodboard

Navigate to the Moodboard section of the app.

👤

Step 3: Access Your Profile

Click on your profile icon to access your profile settings.

❌

Step 4: Delete Account

Scroll down and click on Delete Account. Follow the prompts to confirm.

Important: Once you delete your account, it will be permanently removed within 24 hours.
